# 01. Market / competitor analysis (Australia)

## AU homeowner + tradie marketplace context

Australia’s home-services market is crowded by lead-generation directories and broad task marketplaces. The most relevant competitive set for Fixit247 is:

1. **hipages** — strongest incumbent in Australia for homeowner-to-tradie lead generation, with broad category coverage and a large tradie supply base.
2. **Oneflare** — quote marketplace spanning home services and professional services.
3. **Airtasker** — broader tasking marketplace that overlaps with handyman/light-trade demand, but is less specialised for licensed work.
4. **Google Maps / local Facebook groups / word of mouth** — still the real “default competitor” for many urgent jobs because people want speed and trust, not more listings.

## What the incumbent pattern gets wrong

### 1) Too much directory behaviour, not enough buying intent orchestration
Homeowners typically want to answer one question quickly: **“Who can actually do this job in my area, in my timeframe, at a price I can understand?”** Traditional directories often over-index on volume and under-index on readiness.

### 2) Opaque or inconsistent quote comparison
Many marketplaces surface “contacted” tradies faster than they surface **comparable pricing**. That creates friction, decision fatigue, and distrust.

### 3) Weak retention loops for both sides
Most platforms focus on job acquisition, not repeat behaviour:
- Homeowners are not strongly encouraged to come back for the next job.
- Tradies are not rewarded enough for speed, clarity, or retention behaviour beyond generic lead access.

### 4) Trust is present, but not composed well in the UX
ABN/licence/review signals exist in the market, but the interaction model often makes the homeowner dig for confidence rather than instantly feel it.

## Current positioning vs HiPages

### HiPages strengths
- Strong category breadth and brand recognition in Australia.
- Established tradie network and homeowner familiarity.
- Existing operational maturity around lead delivery and tradie tooling.

### Fixit247 differentiation
Fixit247 should position as:

> **“The faster, clearer, more trusted tradie-booking experience for Australian homeowners — and the more predictable subscription-growth engine for quality tradies.”**

### Strategic differentiators
1. **Speed-to-confidence, not just speed-to-contact**
   - Match by suburb overlap, approval status, category fit, and response-time signals.
   - Show “fast reply” and “can attend today” cues immediately.

2. **Quote clarity as a product feature**
   - Structured quote builder with labour, materials, fees, estimated duration, and start date.
   - Side-by-side homeowner comparison view.

3. **Trust stack visible in one glance**
   - ABN, licence, reviews, reply speed, featured portfolio, service area fit.
   - Admin moderation and dispute tooling reinforce perceived quality.

4. **Subscription-led tradie economics**
   - Fixed monthly lead allowances reduce spend anxiety.
   - Explicit rollover and upgrade/downgrade rules improve fairness and predictability.

5. **Retention loops baked into the product**
   - Homeowner returning-customer code and repeat booking shortcuts.
   - Tradie streaks, fast-reply badges, and profile-lift incentives.

## Suggested marketplace wedge

### Primary launch wedge
Start with **urgent, high-trust, high-response-value categories** in metro Australia:
- Plumbing
- Electrical
- HVAC
- Locksmith / emergency handyman

These categories are ideal because the homeowner pain is immediate and response-time UX matters most.

### Geography wedge
Launch first around dense urban supply in:
- Sydney
- Melbourne
- Brisbane

The included seed data currently uses **Sydney / NSW** to keep the MVP coherent and locally realistic.

## Practical acquisition ideas for AU growth

### Homeowner growth
- SEO landing pages by suburb + category.
- “Book the same tradie again” retention prompts after completion.
- Email + SMS nudges for incomplete quote comparison.

### Tradie growth
- Position subscriptions around **predictable ROI** versus “pay and pray” lead marketplaces.
- Offer a launch trial or first-cycle onboarding support for approved tradies.
- Surface performance badges that directly influence match rank.
